LUNAR LOG

Lunar Log is a volunteer tracking app that allows ease of use for organizers and volunteers to track, manage, and update their volunteer hours.

  • Jayanth
  • Aaditya
  • Vishruth

Jayanth Annabhimoju worked on the backend systems which would print the certificates, manage the database to hold the volunteer hours, dates, and systems to read and authenticate volunteer timings. In addition to this, jayanth worked on the deployment of his own website to the cloud through github pages.

GET CERTIFICATE

Volunteers can find their logs here and also be able to checkout their hours and recieve their certificates

Our main use case for this was to help the people at LEAF Foundations in managing their ever growing volunteer base, and saving them from struggles of thousands of emails asking for volunteer certifications and confirmations. Hopefully our product will allow for the people of tthe LEAF Foundation and personally our friend Tony Singh, to be able to spend more time not looking through such emails.
